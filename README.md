# Prediction-of-Product-Sales

This project focuses on building a predictive model for product sales, leveraging various item and outlet characteristics. The goal is to identify key factors that drive sales performance and to develop a robust model capable of forecasting future sales.

Through comprehensive data analysis, we aim to uncover insights into customer purchasing patterns, product popularity, and the impact of store attributes on sales. This information will be crucial for optimizing inventory, marketing strategies, and overall business operations.
